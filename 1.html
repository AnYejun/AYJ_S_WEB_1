2021 NBA FINAL

   MILWAUKEE BUCKS VS PHOENIX SUNS

                       

 

   Tonight, Milwaukee Bucks fans waited a long time for this. Their patience was rewarded. In the first NBA Finals game in the city in 47 years, the Bucks put together a complete effort and pulled
away for a 120-100 victory over the Phoenix Suns in Game 3 on Sunday at Fiserv Forum. Milwaukee took control with a big run to end the third quarter and the Bucks narrowed Phoenix's advantage
in the series to 2-1 in the best-of-seven series. The Bucks can get back to level in Game 4 at 8 p.m.
Wednesday at Fiserv Forum.
